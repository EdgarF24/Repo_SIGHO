üìä 11 System Tables

  • users - System user management with roles and permissions
  • amenities - Catalog of room amenities/services (WiFi, TV, etc.)
  • room_type_amenities - Many-to-many relationship between room types and amenities
  • room_types - Room types with multi-currency pricing
  • rooms - Physical hotel rooms
  • guests - Hotel guest registry
  • reservations - Complete reservation management (check-in/out)
  • payments - Multi-currency payment control and methods
  • maintenance - Preventive and corrective maintenance
  • inventory - Stock control with alerts
  • inventory_movements - Movement history
